# Knowledge Base Article — Resetting Your Password  
**Acme Cloud Platform (August 2015)**  

## Purpose
This article provides step-by-step instructions for resetting your password on the Acme Cloud Platform.  
It applies to all users who access the system via the web portal.  

---

## When to Use This Guide
- You have forgotten your password.  
- Your account has been locked after too many failed login attempts.  
- Your administrator has instructed you to reset your password.  

---

## Steps to Reset Your Password

### Step 1 — Navigate to the Login Page
1. Open a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer 11).  
2. Go to [https://login.acmecloud.com](https://login.acmecloud.com).  

### Step 2 — Select “Forgot Password”
1. Click the **Forgot Password?** link below the login form.  
2. Enter your registered email address.  
3. Click **Submit**.  

### Step 3 — Check Your Email
1. You will receive an email from **no-reply@acmecloud.com**.  
2. Open the email and click the **Reset Password** link.  
3. If you don’t receive the email within 10 minutes:  
   - Check your spam or junk folder.  
   - Verify that you entered the correct email.  

### Step 4 — Create a New Password
1. Enter a new password that meets the following requirements:  
   - At least 8 characters.  
   - Includes at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, and 1 number.  
   - Cannot match your last 5 passwords.  
2. Confirm the new password.  
3. Click **Save**.  

### Step 5 — Log In
1. Return to the login page.  
2. Enter your username and new password.  
3. Click **Login** to access your account.  

---

## Troubleshooting
- **Error: Link Expired** — Password reset links expire after **30 minutes**. Request a new reset link.  
- **Error: Email Not Found** — Contact your administrator to verify your account is active.  
- **Still Unable to Login** — Contact the Acme Cloud Helpdesk at **support@acmecloud.com**.
